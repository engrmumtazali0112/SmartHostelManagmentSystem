Room Type Information

The room type determines the maximum capacity of the room:

Single to Five Seaters

  • One Seater: 1 student
  • Two Seater: 2 students
  • Three Seater: 3 students
  • Four Seater: 4 students
  • Five Seater: 5 students

Six to Ten Seaters

  • Six Seater: 6 students
  • Seven Seater: 7 students
  • Eight Seater: 8 students
  • Nine Seater: 9 students
  • Ten Seater: 10 students

Note

Initially, no students will be allocated to the new room. You can assign students later through the student allocation interface.
